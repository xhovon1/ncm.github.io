Why You'll Love NoCodeML

All the power, none of the complexity.

On-Device & Private

All training and prediction happens 100% offline. Your data never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Powerful Algorithm Suite

Go beyond simple models. Choose from over 10 industry-standard algorithms, including Random Forest and Gradient Boost.

Robust Cross-Validation

No more guessing. The app automatically performs 5-fold cross-validation to provide reliable, real-world performance metrics.

Hyperparameter Tuning

Fine-tune your models like a pro with intuitive sliders for key parameters like the number of trees or max nodes.

Flexible Prediction

Test your trained models on new CSV files or enter data for a single prediction manually through a simple form.

Export Your Results

Save your prediction results, including the original data and the new "Predicted_Label" column, to a new CSV file with a single tap.
